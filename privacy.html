Video Downloader

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Video Downloader ("the app") is a browser and download manager for Android published by FightTech. This policy explains what the app accesses and how your data is handled. In short: your files stay on your device, and we do not sell your data.

What the app does

The app lets you browse websites, detect a video on a page or paste a link, and save that media to your device. Downloading is initiated by you. The app does not host, provide, or stream any content itself — it fetches the media you choose from the third‑party sites you visit.

Permissions we use

  • Internet (INTERNET, ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE) — to load web pages and download the media you request.
  • Storage / media (READ_MEDIA_VIDEO, READ_MEDIA_AUDIO on Android 13+, or WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE on older versions) — to save downloads to your gallery and to show your video & music library inside the app.
  • Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS) — to show download progress and completion.
  • Foreground service & media playback — to keep downloads and background playback running.

Your files

Downloaded files are saved to your device's public gallery folders (Movies / Music / Pictures) or, if you choose, to the app's PIN‑locked private folder. Your media, download history, and private‑folder PIN are stored locally on your device. We never upload, copy, or sell your files, and there is no account.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics for Firebase to collect anonymous usage statistics (such as which features are used, device model, and app version) to improve the app. This does not identify you personally and does not include the content, URLs, or names of the media you download. See Google's Privacy Policy.

Support messages

If you contact us from the in‑app Support screen, the message you type is sent to our support inbox so we can help you. Please don't include sensitive personal information in support messages.

Advertising

The current version of the app does not display ads. If ads are added in a future version, this policy will be updated to describe the advertising SDK used and your choices, and this app's app-ads.txt at /app-ads.txt declares our authorized sellers.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your responsibility

You are responsible for how you use downloaded content. Only download material you have the right to save, and respect the terms of service and copyright of the sites you visit.
