Description

Video Downloader is the simplest way to save videos and music on Android. Open the built-in browser, go to any page with a video, and a Download button appears the moment a video is detected — one tap and it's saved to your gallery. Or just paste a link. No account, no watermark, no fuss.

Everything you need in one app:

  • Built-in browser that auto-detects video on any page
  • One-tap download — or paste a link / use Share
  • Save HD video or extract audio as MP3
  • Works with TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, X, Pinterest, Vimeo, Reddit & more
  • Download manager — pause, resume & multiple at once
  • Built-in player with background & pop-up play
  • Video & music library, organized automatically
  • Private folder locked with a PIN
  • Fast, lightweight & free
  • No login, no watermark

Video Downloader is a general-purpose browser and downloader. Please only download content you have the right to save, and respect the terms of the sites you visit. The app does not host or provide any content.
